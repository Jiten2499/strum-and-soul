Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to own an instrument before joining?

+

Not at all! You can use our studio instruments during lessons. We'll guide you on what to buy once you're comfortable — and we offer great prices in our shop.

Is there a free trial class?

+

Yes! Every new student gets one free 30-minute demo session. Zero commitment — just come and experience our teaching style firsthand.

What age groups do you teach?

+

We teach students from age 6 to 60+. Our instructors are experienced in adapting their methods for children, teenagers, working adults, and senior learners.

Can I switch between online and offline classes?

+

Absolutely. You can freely mix in-person and live-online sessions. Just inform your instructor 24 hours in advance — no extra charge for switching.

How long does it take to learn guitar?

+

Most beginners play their first full songs within 1–2 months. Confident intermediate playing takes 6–12 months. Advanced skills develop over 2+ years with consistent practice.

What instrument brands do you stock?

+

We carry guitars from Fender, Yamaha, Cort, Ibanez, and Taylor; keyboards from Casio, Roland, and Korg — all with warranty and competitive pricing.

Do you offer certificate courses?

+

Yes! We offer ABRSM-affiliated certificates for guitar, keyboard, and music theory, plus our own in-house certification recognized by partner institutions.

Can I pause my membership if I travel?

+

Yes — up to 4 weeks per year. Inform us 3 days in advance and your subscription period extends accordingly. No questions asked.
