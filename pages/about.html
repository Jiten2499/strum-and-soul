Our Story
"We didn't build a music school. We built a place where music becomes inevitable."
Vikram Nanda
Founder & Director

It began in 2017 with a second-hand guitar, a folding chair, and six students who showed up because a flyer said "learn guitar — any age, any level." Vikram Nanda, a Berklee College of Music graduate who had spent a decade performing across India and Southeast Asia, had returned to Chandigarh with one conviction: that world-class music education didn't have to mean moving to Mumbai or Delhi.

The first year was humble. Lessons were given in a 12-foot room above a stationary shop in Sector 17. By the end of year two, word of mouth had brought 60 students through those doors. The space expanded, the faculty grew, and the instrument shop was born — because too many students were arriving with poorly set-up guitars that made learning twice as hard.

Today, Strum & Soul occupies a dedicated studio space with four teaching rooms, a performance hall, and a fully stocked instrument shop. Our 12 instructors between them hold certifications from Berklee, Trinity College London, ABRSM, and have performed at venues from the Chandigarh Rose Festival to international stages in Singapore and Dubai.

But the philosophy hasn't changed. We believe music is not a talent you either have or don't — it's a skill, a discipline, and ultimately a joy that belongs to anyone willing to show up and practice. Every student who walks through our door deserves a teacher who remembers what it felt like to play their first note.

What Drives Us

Built on Six Pillars

🎯

Student-First, Always

Every decision we make — from how we structure a lesson to which instruments we stock — begins with a single question: what serves the student best? Not what's easiest for us, not what looks best on a brochure. The student's musical growth is the only metric that matters.

🏆

Excellence Without Elitism

World-class teaching shouldn't require a world-class budget. We price our courses fairly and make premium music education genuinely accessible to students from all walks of life.

🌱

Progress Over Perfection

We celebrate the messy middle — the barre chord that almost works, the scale run that's nearly there. Confidence grows from consistent progress, not impossible standards.

🌐

Music Has No Borders

Our curriculum spans Western classical, Hindustani, jazz, rock, pop, and film music. We believe a musician who understands many traditions is richer than one who masters only one.

🤝

Community Over Competition

Our students don't compete — they collaborate. Regular ensemble sessions, jam nights, and group showcases turn individual learners into a musical family.

🔥

Instructors Who Still Perform

Every instructor at Strum & Soul is an active performer — not just an academic. They bring the stage into the classroom: the real energy of live music, the practical wisdom of a working musician, and the honest truth about what separates good playing from great playing.

Arjun Sharma

Lead Guitar Instructor & Head of Strings
Berklee Certified ABRSM Grade 8 14 Years Teaching Live Performer

Arjun Sharma is the heart of Strum & Soul's guitar programme. Trained at Berklee College of Music in Boston on a merit scholarship, he spent five years performing across India, Singapore, and Dubai before returning to Chandigarh with a vision: to bring world-class guitar instruction to his home city. His teaching philosophy is built on three pillars — solid technique, deep ear training, and above all, the joy of making music. He has trained over 180 students ranging from 8-year-old beginners to 60-year-old retirees picking up a guitar for the first time. Arjun specialises in rock, blues, fingerpicking, and progressive styles, and his advanced students have gone on to perform at national-level competitions and one has signed with an independent record label. When he's not teaching, he fronts the Chandigarh-based jazz-rock quartet The Nanda Collective.

Vocals

Vocals · Classical · Pop · Hindustani

Priya Nair

Senior Vocal Coach & Head of Voice
Pt. Jasraj Lineage Trinity Licentiate 11 Years Teaching Vocalist & Composer

Priya Nair's voice is the kind that makes a room go quiet. Trained under disciples of the legendary Pandit Jasraj in the Mewati gharana tradition, she later pursued contemporary vocal techniques through Trinity College London, earning a Licentiate in Music for voice performance. Her dual mastery of Hindustani classical and Western contemporary styles makes her uniquely equipped to teach the whole range of what the human voice can do. Priya's teaching method prioritises vocal health above all — she believes a damaged voice is a musician's greatest loss, and every session includes work on breathing, posture, and sustainable technique. Her 220+ students include professional singers, Bollywood backup vocalists, and complete beginners who simply always wanted to sing. She runs monthly "Sing Without Fear" workshops specifically designed to help students overcome performance anxiety and find their authentic voice.

Keys & Theory
📸 ▶️
Keyboard · Piano · Jazz Theory · Composition

Rahul Verma

Keyboard & Music Theory Instructor
Trinity Grade 8 ABRSM Theory Grade 8 9 Years Teaching Jazz Ensemble Pianist

Rahul Verma has a gift that few music educators possess: the ability to make music theory not just comprehensible, but genuinely exciting. Holding Trinity Grade 8 performance certification and ABRSM Grade 8 theory, he is the backbone of Strum & Soul's keyboard curriculum and the reason our students actually understand why a chord progression sounds the way it does. An active performer with the City Jazz Ensemble, Rahul brings the living, breathing world of jazz harmony into his lessons — teaching chord substitutions, modal improvisation, and voice leading in ways that connect to real musical situations. He has a particular talent for working with adult beginners who believe they've "left it too late," and for children aged 5–12 who are just discovering the magic of keys. His signature "Harmony Workshop" — a monthly group session open to all keyboard and guitar students — has become one of Strum & Soul's most beloved events.

Drums
📸 ▶️
Drums · Rock · Jazz · Funk · Latin

Dev Malhotra

Drums & Percussion Instructor
Studio Session Drummer Live Touring Performer 10 Years Teaching 100+ Sessions Recorded

Dev Malhotra is the kind of drummer other musicians love playing with — locked in, musical, and always serving the song. After a decade touring with bands across North India and recording session drums for over 100 tracks (from Bollywood to indie rock), he joined Strum & Soul to pass on everything he'd learned the hard way. Dev's approach to teaching is deceptively simple: first, make it feel good; then, make it perfect. He prioritises internal time over click-track dependence, groove over complexity, and listening over showing off. His students cover the full spectrum — 8-year-olds on beginner kits, working musicians looking to tighten their groove, and adult hobbyists who just want to finally learn to play along to their favourite songs. Dev runs monthly Ensemble Jam Sessions where drum students play alongside Strum & Soul's guitar and keyboard students — giving everyone an experience that is much closer to real musical life than a practice room ever can be.
