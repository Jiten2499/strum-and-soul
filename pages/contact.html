Quick Answers

Is the demo class really free?
Yes — completely. Every new student gets one 30-minute demo session at no cost and with zero commitment. Just show up, play, and see if we're the right fit.

Book your free demo →
Do I need to bring my own instrument?
Not for the first few lessons. We have studio instruments you can use while you're getting started. We'll guide you on what to buy (and offer great prices in our shop) when you're ready.

Browse our instrument shop →
How quickly can you repair my guitar?
Minor setups (action, intonation, string change) are usually done same-day. Electronics repairs and fret work take 2–5 days. Full restorations are quoted individually. Walk-ins welcome.
